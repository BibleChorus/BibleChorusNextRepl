# Chapter 7: Doxology

*A Scripture-rich eruption of praise to the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, awakening holy fear and grateful love.*

## Coming Soon

This final chapter will be a worship-focused conclusion that magnifies God's character and works. We will praise:

### The Father's Holiness
- His absolute purity and hatred of sin
- His righteous judgment and perfect justice
- His amazing patience and kindness toward sinners
- His sovereign plan of redemption

### The Son's Sacrifice
- Christ's perfect obedience where we failed
- His substitutionary death for sinners
- His resurrection power over sin and death
- His ongoing intercession for believers

### The Spirit's Power
- Convicting the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment
- Regenerating dead hearts
- Sanctifying believers progressively
- Empowering victory over sin

### Praise for God's Character
- **Holiness** - "Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty"
- **Justice** - His perfect righteousness in all His ways
- **Mercy** - His compassion toward the repentant
- **Grace** - His undeserved favor toward sinners
- **Love** - The motivation behind our salvation

### Worship and Wonder
A Scripture-saturated conclusion that leaves readers:
- Amazed at God's holiness
- Humbled by their own sinfulness  
- Grateful for Christ's salvation
- Motivated to holy living
- Confident in God's power to deliver

*"Oh, the depth of the riches and wisdom and knowledge of God! How unsearchable are his judgments and how inscrutable his ways!"* (<BibleVerse reference="Romans 11:33" />)
